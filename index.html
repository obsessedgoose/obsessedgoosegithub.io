About Me

My name is Kyle McDonald. I am a hard-working person that does not like to sit idle. I've found that
when it comes down to crunch time, I roll up my sleeves and dive in. Finding myself wading into problems
and trying to sort them out. I work well with a team that is well oiled and I like to think of those I work
with as family. I started my life out busting semi tires and worked my way into management there. I have never
forgotten how hard work pays off, whether it be mental or physical. I find satisfaction at the end of a day when
I feel as if I built or achomplished something; sometimes not finding rest until I do.
